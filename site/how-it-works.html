How it works

From scroll-paralysis to actual plans.

IRLobby cuts the back-and-forth. You answer a few quick questions, swipe on stuff that's actually happening near you, and chat the moment someone matches.

1 Vibe Quiz

Tell us your vibe in 60 seconds.

Five fast cards, no boring forms. Are you in the mood for chaos or chill? Solo recharge or full-blown party? Your answers tune the activities you'll see — and they're saved for next time.

  • ● Swipeable card UX, not a survey
  • ● Skip and edit any answer later
  • ● Results work offline
⚡
2 Discover

Swipe on plans, not selfies.

Every card is a real activity — tonight's open mic, tomorrow's hike, Friday's game night. You see what it is, who's hosting, where, and how soon. Swipe right if you're in.

  • ● Filter by distance + time
  • ● Tags for vibe at a glance
  • ● Or host your own activity in seconds
🎯
3 Match & Meet

Mutual swipe = instant chat.

When someone else swipes your activity, confetti drops and chat unlocks. Coordinate where to meet, what to bring, when to bail if it rains. Then actually show up.

  • ● Real-time messaging
  • ● Activity context built into every chat
  • ● Block + report if anyone's off
🎉
