Common questions

  • I didn't get a verification email. Check spam/junk. Re-request from the sign-in screen.
  • I want to delete my account. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, matches, and messages.
  • Activities aren't showing up. Make sure location access is enabled in iOS / Android settings.
  • Reporting a person or activity. Tap the ⋯ menu on any profile or activity card.
