Features

Every detail tuned for getting out the door.

No endless feeds, no algorithm rabbit holes. Just a tight loop: discover something fun, match, meet up.

⚡

Vibe Quiz

5 swipeable cards in 60 seconds. The quiz tunes your discover feed and persists offline so you don't have to redo it on every cold start.

🎯

Activity-first discover

Cards are real plans, not profiles. See what it is, when, where, and how far. Swipe right to express interest.

🎉

Match celebration

Mutual swipe triggers confetti, haptics, and a heart pulse — with reduced-motion respected for accessibility.

💬

Real-time chat

WebSocket-backed messaging with the activity context attached, so the conversation never loses what you're meeting up for.

📍

Hyper-local

Distance-aware ranking with Mapbox tiles. You only see things you can actually get to in time.

🛜

Offline-ready

Last quiz results and recent matches stay available with no signal. Syncs back up automatically when you're connected.

🛡️

Safety baked in

Block and report from any profile or chat. Email-verified accounts. Optional phone verification for extra signal.

🌗

Looks great in dark mode

Designed for OLED first. Auto-follows your system theme, with an explicit override in settings.
